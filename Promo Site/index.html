Introducing your iPod, like you've never seen it before.

Re-designed from the ground up, adwaitapod provides a modern, feature rich experience on your iPod Classic. Designed around the GNOME Project's Human Interface Guidelines, adwaitapod is built with a clean, user-friendly interface at it's core.

See What's New

Two Styles

Classic

The classic adwaitapod player. Provides you with all the playback information you could need. Including playtime, playlist position, and icons for lossless music, explicit songs and playback modes.

  • Compact Menus.
  • Information-Rich Player.
  • FM Radio Player.

Simplified

The new, redesigned adwaitapod player. With a focus on the album art, this player gives a clean and simple experience. Letting you enjoy your music without too much information getting in the way.

  • Spacious Menus.
  • Minimal, Album-Art focused Player.
  • No FM Radio Player.

Make it your own

Choose from Light and Dark modes.

Each style comes in a Light and Dark variant. Letting you select a colourscheme that suits your tastes, or even swap on the fly as the day turns to night.

Select from 8 different Wallpapers.

With the Extra Wallpapers Pack, you can select and install 8 different Wallpapers. Each Wallpaper comes with a Light and Dark variant, for a total of 16 unique Wallpapers.

Tweak adwaitapod further with User Preferences.

The new User Preferences Settings allow each user to change adwaitapod's behaviour to their tastes. Preferences include making Album Art square instead of rounded, toggle to show the Artist's name or Album title in the player, and more!

43 Supported Languages

Use adwaitapod in your Language

adwaitapod makes use of Rockbox's existing translations to support 43 different languages. As much of the UI is translated as possible, please check the wiki for more detailed information.
